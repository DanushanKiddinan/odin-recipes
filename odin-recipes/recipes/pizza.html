Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish made with a wheat flour dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, and baked in an oven until the crust is crispy.

To make pizza at home, you will need to start by preheating your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Next, you will need to prepare your dough by mixing together flour, water, yeast, and salt until a smooth dough forms. Allow the dough to rise for about an hour, or until it has doubled in size. Once the dough is ready, roll it out into a large circle and place it on a baking sheet or pizza pan. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edge. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, and then add any desired toppings, such as vegetables, meats, or herbs. Finally, bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread the pesto on pizza crust.
  3. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned, 8-10 minutes.
