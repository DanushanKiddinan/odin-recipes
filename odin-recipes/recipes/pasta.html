Pasta

Pasta is a versatile and delicious food that can be enjoyed in a variety of dishes, from classic spaghetti and meatballs to creamy pasta alfredo and refreshing pasta salads.

A pasta recipe is a set of instructions for preparing a dish that includes pasta as a main ingredient. There are many different types of pasta dishes, ranging from simple and quick meals like spaghetti with marinara sauce to more complex and time-consuming dishes like lasagna or ravioli. Pasta recipes can be made with a variety of ingredients, such as vegetables, meats, seafood, cheeses, and sauces, and can be served hot or cold. Some popular pasta recipes include spaghetti carbonara, baked ziti, and fettuccine alfredo. Pasta recipes are often easy to prepare and can be a tasty and satisfying meal for people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) Package Linguine Pasta
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 Shallots, Finely Diced
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Pinch Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Pound Shrimp, Peeled and Deveined
  • 1 Pinch Kosher Salt and Freshly Ground
  • 1/2 Cup Dry White Wine
  • 1 Lemon, Juiced
  • 1/4 Cup Finely Chopped Fresh Parsely Leaves
  • 1 Teaspoon Extra-Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; cook linguine in boiling water until nearly tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter with 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir shallots, garlic, and red pepper flakes in the hot butter and oil until shallots are translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Season shrimp with kosher salt and black pepper; add to the skillet and cook until pink, stirring occasionally, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove shrimp from skillet and keep warm.
  4. Pour white wine and lemon juice into skillet and bring to a boil while scraping the browned bits of food off of the bottom of the skillet with a wooden spoon.
  5. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet, stir 2 tablespoons olive oil into butter mixture, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Toss linguine, shrimp, and parsley in the butter mixture until coated; season with salt and black pepper. Drizzle with 1 teaspoon olive oil to serve.
